This summer we will be teaching software craftsmanship to ambitious women and men in Palestine.

Where traditional industries are limited, software is a viable alternative. Fast internet and good equipment open the door to a global marketplace, where work can be both fulfilling and profitable.

In London, the Founders & Coders educational model sees 100% of its graduates go on to be full-stack JavaScript developers or tech entrepreneurs. This summer, Globetown, F&C and Novus will travel over 2000 miles and repeat this success with students and graduates at Birzeit University, in Palestine.
